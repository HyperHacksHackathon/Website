FAQ

Who can apply?

HyperHacks is open to all high school and university students.

Where is it taking place?

HyperHacks will be happening on discord.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is and event where participants/hackers work in teams to bring a project idea life. For HyperHacks, hackers will have 48 hours to complete their projects. Hackers can receive mentorship, learn about job opportunities, and get the chance to win awesome prizes!
